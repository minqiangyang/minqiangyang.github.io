Dr. Minqiang Yang, a member of IEEE, ACM, CCF, and CSIG, is a researcher affiliated with the School of Information Science and Engineering at Lanzhou University. He holds a Ph.D. and a master's degree from Lanzhou University, as well as a bachelor's degree from Soochow University. Dr. Yang's research interests encompass affective computing, machine learning, medical informatics, and mental disorder detection. He has authored approximately 20 papers and received several prestigious awards, including the ACM Lanzhou Outstanding Doctoral Graduate Award in 2022, the EAI MobiHealth 2021 Best Paper Award, and the IEEE Healthcom 2020 Best Service Award. Dr. Yang is dedicated to conducting research on the quantitative assessment of mental disorders and autism, specifically focusing on facial behavior. His ultimate goal is to facilitate the widespread application of universal devices within these industries.
